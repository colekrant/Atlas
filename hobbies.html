Hobbies

One of my favorite hobbies is hiking. I absolutely love escaping into nature, especially with a group of friends. As an officer of the Hiking Club at CU, I am priviledged with the oppourtunity to lead strangers on adventures throughout the mountains.

Being from Texas, we don't see much snow. When I came to Colorado for college, I decided to try snowboarding. My roommate was a snowboard instructor and taught me everything I know. I absolutely love shredding the mountains and am excited for this season.

Mountain Biking is a hobby I began in Texas and have struggled to bring into Colorado. The terrain and elevation make things far more difficult than what I'm used to. None the less, I always enjoy the thrill that comes with Mountain Biking.
